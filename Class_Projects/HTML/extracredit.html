Dos and Don'ts of creating a webpage

Things to do

  • Make sure text is easy to read and well grouped/spaced
  • Add color or images that go together well and do not impede text readibality.
  • Make sure it is clear whether something is a link or not
  • Use a professional and easy to read font
  • Consider users accessing the page on different platforms

Things to avoid

  • Avoid sound that may suddenly start playing without user input(such as from ads)
  • Do not fill the page with too many ads
  • Avoid having too many gifs/graphics that may distract the user or slow down the page
  • Do not go off topic and clutter the screen with useless information
  • Avoid having huge chunks of text
